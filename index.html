Project 1: Metroid NES Remake

Metroid was a game series produced by Nintendo and debuted in Japan for the Famicom Disk System in 1986. It was re-released for the Game Boy Advance as Classic NES Series: Metroid.

In this project, I worked with my teammate - Salvatore Ciatti - to remake portions of the original game in an authentic way. Later, we also added a new custom level - Dark Mode Metroid - in our remake, creating fun and refreshing experiences for fans and players.

Project 2: Shuriken: Die For Ramen

Shuriken: Die For Ramen is a 2D-Platformer game I developed from scratch for my project 2 in EECS 494 in 2 weeks.

In this game, you will play as a ninja who've spent his life questing for the ultimate flavor of ramen. The journey is full of obstacles and dangers. Luckily, you are borned with the most mysterious Ninjutsu which has been lost for thoudsands of years among the Ninja!
